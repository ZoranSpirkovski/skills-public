# archive-1-1-welcome

## Summary

Lesson 1.1 opens The Archive (also called Old But Gold), Van Clief's retrospective course. The course asks one question about the videos, articles, and frameworks he produced before most people knew what a context window was: did it hold up? Mostly yes. The core ideas landed, the predictions tracked, and the frameworks evolved into the methodology he teaches today. The course has three modules. Module 1 frames the history of thinking machines. Module 2 pairs three late-2023 and early-2024 videos with 2026 written companions. Module 3 collects current articles that show where the early thinking now points.

## Key claims

1. The Archive is a retrospective test of Van Clief's pre-2024 work against present reality, with honest revisions treated as a feature rather than an embarrassment.
2. The course spans three modules: Setup (history frame), Old But Gold (three paired video-plus-companion retrospectives), and Where It Goes Now (four current articles).
3. Module 2 covers The Mindset Before the Method (Flagler College workshop, January 2024), Raise the Bar (December 2023), and More Human (December 2023).
4. Module 3 covers Beyond the Turing Test, The Questions Schools Should Be Asking, The Real Cost of Knowledge (the apocalypse-laptop thought experiment), and The Rise of Computational Orchestration.
5. The videos in Module 2 sit inside a 2,000-year pattern of building thinking machines, normalizing them, and forgetting they were ever impossible.
6. New students should watch The Foundation first. The Archive adds depth and lineage to what The Foundation teaches.
