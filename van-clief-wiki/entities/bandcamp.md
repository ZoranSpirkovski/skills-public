# bandcamp

Music distribution and sales platform. Van Clief cites it as the working proof that musicians can own their distribution directly rather than surrender it to a label. Used in Lesson 3.2 as one of three precedent industries (alongside GitHub for code and Substack for writing) for the ownership-in-education model he proposes.

## Claims

- Proof that musicians can own their distribution channel directly [sources/archive-3-2-questions-schools-should-be-asking]
