# van-clief

Matthew Creamer, working under the name Van Clief. Teacher and community builder focused on AI-native folder workflows. Creator of the Clief Notes community (~12,500 members), author of "The Foundation", "The Archive / Old But Gold", "Implementation Playbooks", and "Building Your Stack" courses. First-person voice throughout the corpus. Background includes teaching AI in education and business "for quite a few years before most people knew what a context window was."
