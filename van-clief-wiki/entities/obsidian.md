# obsidian

Markdown-based knowledge-base / vault application. Mentioned in the folder-system talk as a suitable environment for working inside the folder methodology. Its native handling of wikilinks and graph view is a natural fit for a markdown-first workflow.

## Claims

- Noted as a reading/working environment for folder-based markdown workflows [sources/folder-system-transcript]
- Renders markdown plus graph views and mind maps of README/context relationships; positioned as an advanced tool, not essential for early users [sources/playbooks-1-5-claude-design-folder-structure]
- Van Clief recommends Obsidian "as you navigate these structures, as you get better in understanding software engineering" rather than up-front [sources/playbooks-1-5-claude-design-folder-structure]
