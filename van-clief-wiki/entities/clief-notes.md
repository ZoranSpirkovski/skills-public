# clief-notes

Van Clief's Skool community for AI practitioners. Grew out of a 2023 project originally called Quantum Quill, conceived as a forum for educators to discuss AI in the classroom seriously. Now sits at 12,500+ members. Van Clief positions Clief Notes as the answer to the "community void" problem: the original vacuum was real, but most of what filled it since 2023 has been tool-focused hype rather than serious work.

## Claims

- Founded to address the "community void" problem from the Flagler workshop thesis [sources/archive-2-1-mindset-before-method]
- Originally called Quantum Quill and focused on educators; now broader in scope [sources/archive-2-1-mindset-before-method]
- 12,500+ members as of 2026 [sources/archive-2-1-mindset-before-method]
- Van Clief frames it as "the community doing this seriously" in contrast to tool-focused or hype-driven AI communities [sources/archive-2-1-mindset-before-method]
- Hosts Van Clief's courses including The Foundation and The Archive / Old But Gold [sources/archive-2-1-mindset-before-method] [sources/archive-2-2-raise-the-bar] [sources/archive-2-3-more-human]
