# eduba

Van Clief's training and tooling organization. Originally described in 2023 as a tool project (the "Duba") aimed at solving the hard-to-use, non-education-focused nature of available AI tools. It has since grown into three product lines that map onto the three structural problems Van Clief identified in the original Flagler workshop. Training workshops address the AI overwhelm. EdubaWare addresses the tool gap. Clief Notes, the community, addresses the community void.

## Claims

- Founded to address three structural problems: AI overwhelm, tool deficiency, community void [sources/archive-2-1-mindset-before-method]
- Three product lines now: training workshops, EdubaWare tooling, and Clief Notes community [sources/archive-2-1-mindset-before-method]
- Has trained 600+ professionals across Pacific Life, Colgate-Palmolive, KPMG UK, and others [sources/archive-2-1-mindset-before-method] [sources/archive-2-2-raise-the-bar] [sources/archive-2-3-more-human]
- Teaches computational orchestration as its core methodology [sources/archive-2-2-raise-the-bar]
