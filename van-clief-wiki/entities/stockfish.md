# stockfish

Open-source chess engine, the world's strongest traditional chess engine at the time Archive 1.2 was written. Cited as the baseline that [[alphazero]] surpassed while searching ~900x fewer positions - evidence for the claim that AlphaZero was "understanding better, not calculating more."

## Claims

- Searched ~70 million positions per second; AlphaZero searched ~80,000 per second yet played stronger [sources/archive-1-2-2000-year-overnight-success]

## Relationships

- Baseline surpassed by [[alphazero]]
