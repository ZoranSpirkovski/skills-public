# mount-fuji

Mountain in Japan. Site of the personal story that anchors Archive Lesson 2.3 (More Human). In 2019, while stationed with the Marine Corps in Japan, Van Clief climbed Fuji for a third time during a typhoon. Off the Subarashi trail he found an older hiker with a broken ankle, broken ribs, and severe hypothermia who had been down for four hours. Van Clief and his friend dragged the man up the mountain to a shrine, where the workers saved his life. The experience becomes Van Clief's touchstone for the unrepeatable moments that distinguish human presence from anything a model can generate.
