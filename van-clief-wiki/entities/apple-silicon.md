# apple-silicon

Apple's M-series processors. Referenced in Lesson 3.3 as the engineering platform that makes the apocalypse-laptop thought experiment plausible. An M-series MacBook Air draws 3 to 5W at idle and about 15W in normal use. Adding a local language model brings the total to about 53W, which is where the 9.4-hour solar-powered AI runtime number comes from.

## Claims

- M-series MacBook Air draws 3 to 5W at idle, roughly 15W under normal use [sources/archive-3-3-real-cost-of-knowledge]
- Adding a local language model adds about 38W to that baseline [sources/archive-3-3-real-cost-of-knowledge]
- Efficient enough that a 100W solar panel producing 500 watt-hours per day yields 33 hours of Wikipedia use or 9.4 hours of local AI use [sources/archive-3-3-real-cost-of-knowledge]
