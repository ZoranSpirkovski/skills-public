# obs

OBS (Open Broadcaster Software): free screen-recording tool. Mentioned in Playbooks Lesson 1.1 as an alternative to Remotion's render pipeline: screen-record the Remotion Studio preview instead of going through the render step. Avoids codec issues for users who hit render-environment problems.

## Claims

- Alternative to running Remotion's render process; screen-record the preview instead to sidestep codec problems [sources/playbooks-1-1-building-animations-with-claude-code]
