# ke-jie

Chinese Go grandmaster, world #1 ranking since 2014. Defeated 3-0 by [[alphago]] in 2017. Remembered for his reflection after the match: after humanity spent thousands of years improving its tactics, the machines "tell us that humans are completely wrong" - not a single human has touched the edge of Go's truth.

## Claims

- World #1 Go player since 2014 [sources/archive-1-2-2000-year-overnight-success]
- Lost 3-0 to AlphaGo in 2017; cried after losing [sources/archive-1-2-2000-year-overnight-success]
- "After humanity spent thousands of years improving our tactics, computers tell us that humans are completely wrong. I would go as far as to say not a single human has touched the edge of the truth of Go." [sources/archive-1-2-2000-year-overnight-success]
