# roger-bacon

13th-century English friar and philosopher. Van Clief cites him briefly in the Flagler workshop as a medieval link in the chain of thinkers on artificial persons and automata. In his *Opus Majus* Bacon discusses what Van Clief paraphrases as "artificial persons", sitting between Aristotle's autonomous-tool question and Descartes' later machine-versus-human tests.
