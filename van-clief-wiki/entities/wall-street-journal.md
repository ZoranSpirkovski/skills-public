# wall-street-journal

US financial newspaper. In late 2023 the WSJ ran an article on AI and PhD dissertations that became a reference point in Van Clief's early work. Van Clief wrote a response article titled "AI in Academia: A Tool, a Threat, or a Mirror?" which he read aloud during the December 2023 "More Human" video. The response argues that AI in thesis creation should be expected, not banned, and reframes common objections as fears about the system rather than the tool.

## Claims

- Ran a late-2023 article on AI-generated PhD dissertations that drew strong reader objections [sources/archive-2-3-more-human]
- Reader responses quoted in the article include concerns that AI "devalues years of hard work" and disrespects "centuries of scholarship" [sources/archive-2-3-more-human]
- Van Clief's reply article reframed both objections as revealing insecurity and systemic flaws rather than legitimate problems with AI [sources/archive-2-3-more-human]
