# flagler-college

Liberal arts college in St. Augustine, Florida. Van Clief's alma mater for political science. In January 2024 he gave a 39-minute workshop to Flagler professors on AI in education. That workshop became the source for Archive Lesson 2.1 (The Mindset Before the Method). The college also accepted Van Clief's paper (which used AI models as a co-author in a transparent, documented way) for publishing in the Saints Academic Review.
